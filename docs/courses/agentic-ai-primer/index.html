The series
Read the volume you need; skim the rest as a map of the territory.

Each volume is a self-contained primer — a one-sitting read on a single dimension of agentic AI. Foundations and reasoning engines first; then the building blocks (prompts, tools, memory, planning); then composition, evaluation, security and governance; then the regulatory and applied banking volumes that turn the patterns into real deployments. Read in order if you want the full arc, or jump straight to the volume that matches the question on your desk.

How to read this series

Volumes are independent. Each opens in its own page, designed for a single-sitting read (~25–40 min). The companion Building Agentic AI Systems course is the builder-track deep dive on the same material; this primer series is the breadth read for practitioners and leaders.
